About

Unbothered Wolves is a collection of 5000 algorithmically assembled wolf PFP characters with nostalgic cartoon vibes. These NFTs are stored on the Cardano Blockchain. Each one is unique, but they all have one thing in common, they love to “Chill Out. Stay Unbothered.” Note: There are 50 wolves being withheld from sale; reserved for giveaways, event rewards and team members.
