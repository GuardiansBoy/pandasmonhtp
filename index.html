About

- Clay Pandas Is a 2500 collection of clay PFP characters that is integrated with a humanistic and zombie approach. Each one of the NFTs are unique and stored within the Cardano Blockchain. Each Pandas will be made from 3D rendered clay, pushing creativity with each Clay Pandas.

Lore

Master Shi-Fu has been trying to revive the Pandas from clay since he was born. He was destined to revive the mystical Clay Pandas clan, which was said to be a 2000 years old clan focused on bringing peace to the world. The world has to be cleansed, they said. The world has turned to corruption and evil deeds. Finding the mystical bamboo has turned everything around, and Master Shi Fu has revived the Clay Pandas clan from the dead! They still look a little zombified but does this mean peace? We will soon find out.

Clay Pandas' vision is to be the lead 3D clay-rendered project within the CNFT space; there are tons of substantial clay projects within Cardano, such as Clay Nation, Clayaveras, etc., but 3D-rendered clay we believe is a new type of art we can excel in. We will bring innovation to the space, including our titan series scarred and dark pandas collection soon to come. PFPs represents a unique identity in the space, and We are excited to bring our own designs to the plate.
